PULSE WIDTH MODULATOR
Specifications

You are to design the following:

The circuit will take an analog voltage and convert it to a pulse width.
1. The input will range from 1 to 10V
2. The output pulse will range from 10 microseconds corresponding to 1 V
    to 1 microsecond corresponding to 10 V.
3. The circuit will be discrete and have two inputs.
    A. The control input
    B. The trigger input
4. The maximum speed of operation must be faster than the smallest output pulse.
